Disguise

Disguise is the fifth studio album by American heavy metal band Motionless in White. It was released on June 7, 2019 through Roadrunner Records and was produced by Drew Fulk and Chris "Motionless" Cerulli. It`s the second release on a major label since the band's departure from Fearless Records.It is the band's first album with Vinny Mauro on drums and former Ice Nine Kills guitarist Justin Morrow.
