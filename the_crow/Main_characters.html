Eric Draven

Eric Draven is the main protagonist of The Crow.

He was played by the late martial arts actor Brandon Lee who was accidently killed on set of the film on March 31, 1993.

Shelly Webster

Was the fiancé of Eric Draven, the protagonist of The Crow portrayed by Sofia Shinas

Sarah

Sarah was a friend of Eric Draven and Shelly Webster, and was given Shelly's engagement ring when Eric returns from the dead for revenge.

She was played by Rochelle Davis

Daryl Albreth

Darryl Albrecht is the officer who covered the case of Eric Draven and Shelly Webster’s murders. He is portrayed by Ernie Hudson

Top Dollar

Top Dollar is a criminal that T-Bird's gang works for, as well as the main antagonist.His criminal empire consists of drugs, serial arson and an anarchist view on Devil’s Night.

He is portrayed by Michael Wincott.

T-Bird

T- Bird was the leader of a gang that resided in a neighborhood in Detroit, Michigan. He led his gang in the assault and subsequent murders of Eric Draven and Shelly Webster.

T- Bird is portrayed by David Patrick Kelly

Skank

Skank is a member of the gang led by Top Dollar.

Portrayed by Angel David.

Funboy

Funboy was a morphine drug dealer one of Eric's killers and T-Bird's right-hand man. Funboy is portrayed by Michael Massee
